Game Instructions

Master the art of deception or sharpen your intuition. Learn how to play in both Online and Offline environments.

settings_suggest Core Mechanics

vpn_key

Preparation

The system assigns a Secret Word to all players except one. The player without a word is the Impostor.

chat_bubble

Giving Hints

In clockwise order, every player provides exactly one word that describes their secret word without making it too obvious.

how_to_vote

The Vote

Discuss the hints and cast your vote for the player you suspect is lying. The player with the most votes is eliminated.

emoji_events

Victory Conditions

Crew: Identify the Impostor.
Impostor: Survive until only 2 players remain or guess the Secret Word if caught.

priority_high Special Rule: The 2-Win Streak

  • check_circle

    If the Impostor wins two consecutive games, they achieve a "Mastermind Streak".

  • check_circle

    A streak rewards double leaderboard points and unlocks exclusive vanity icons for the next lobby.

  • check_circle

    Breaking an Impostor's streak as a crew member grants a "Buster Bonus" of +50 points.
